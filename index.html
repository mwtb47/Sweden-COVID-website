This website contains a summary of information from Folkhälsomyndigheten, Socialstyrelsen and Statistiska centralbyrån. The country comparisons use data from the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University and The Economist. Country populations are from The World Bank.

The graphs are interactive. Some have a dropdown to the top-right of the graph which allows selection of other plots. Individual countries can be added and removed from the graph be clicking on them once in the graph legend. The country can be isolated by double clicking on it.
